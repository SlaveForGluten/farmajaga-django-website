About us:

Jaga Farm is a family-run, ecological farm dealing in goat breeding and production of goat's cheese. We sell as part of agricultural retail trade. From the very beginning, our business philosophy has been based on two main pillars: love for animals and the highest quality of offered products. Our goats have access only to healthy, natural and organic food - according to the principle: "Happy goats give excellent milk". We have also given up the participation of any middlemen in the distribution of our products - our cheeses can only be bought from us. This allows us to maintain total control over quality and price.

Our activity began on 2011. When tired of the hustle and bustle of the city, we decided to look for a better place to live. And we found - an abandoned farm in Zakamień near Pińczów. It is a quiet and peaceful refuge located within Natura 2000 in the immediate vicinity of the Pieczyska Nature Reserve - an ideal place to live and work. In 2012, the first goats appeared and we started to produce cheese.

Goat breeding and cheese making are in our case a continuation of family tradition. Started by our grandmother and great-grandmother during harsh post-war times, was a way to make ends meet. Thanks to them we had access to old, tested recipes. This, plus a bit of talent and a lot of passion, allowed us to quickly establish a solid position first in Świętokrzyskie, and then on the domestic healthy food market.

Awards:

June 2014 - for the first time we submitted our cheese to the "Tastes of the Regions" competition organized under the auspices of the Marshal Office in Kielce and we won the first prize.

June 2014 - we have been awarded Incorporation to the Culinary Heritage Świętokrzyskie network as part of the European Network of Regional Culinary Heritage.

September 2015 - we were honored with the "Pearl of 2015" - award for the best Polish regional food product.

June 2016 - our cheese won the Kielce "Tastes of the Regions" competition again.

August 2016 - we have been awarded by the Minister of Agriculture with the medal "Distinguished for Agriculture".

September 2016 - we were chosen the best Organic Farm in the Świętokrzyskie Voivodship.

June 2017 - another first prize in the "Tastes of the Regions" competition.

June 2018 - first place in the fresh cheese category at the Polish Cheese Awards Grand Prix 2018 in Sandomierz.
